DAOFactory

inits a dao based on the think tank config db_type and $dao_mapping definitions. db_type is defined in webapp/config.inc.php as:

$THINKTANK_CFG['db_type'] = 'somedb';

Example: DAOFactory::getDAO('SomeDAO');
